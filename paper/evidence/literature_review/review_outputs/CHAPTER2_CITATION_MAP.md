## Wording Controls

- Use `within the reviewed corpus` for absence or gap claims.
- Use `can`, `may`, or `provides an explanation candidate` when discussing the explanatory role of a path.
- Keep native-path generation, post-hoc attribution, ranking utility, and explanation-quality metrics as separate concepts.
- Do not cite external literature as evidence for this repository's six-model results, alpha sweeps, ablation, or Amazon-Book boundary status.
